Extruder Start G-Code
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning
G1 E270 F10000 ;Extrude new filament to nozzle
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning

Extruder End G-Code
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning
G1 E-7 F4500 ;Retract filament 7 mm
G1 X40 Y65 F7200 ;Move to filament change location
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning
G1 E4 F10000 ;Extrude 2 filament 4 mm
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning
G1 E-270 F5000 ;Retract filament to resting spot
G92 E0 ;Reset extruder positioning
